About Us

Direct Farm to Customer Supply

We are a direct wheat supplier connecting farmers across India with customers nationwide. By eliminating middlemen, we ensure the best prices for our customers while providing fair compensation to farmers.

Our network spans across major wheat-producing regions including Punjab, Haryana, Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan, and Bihar. We work directly with farmer cooperatives to source premium quality wheat varieties.
